The story

Why I built Phuket Living.

I moved to Phuket in 2017 with my partner, two suitcases, and a long list of questions nobody could answer properly. We ended up in the wrong area for six months — a beautiful property, an entirely wrong lifestyle fit. The kids were unsettled, the school run was a daily ordeal, and we kept wondering whether the move had been a mistake.

When we eventually found the right neighbourhood, everything shifted. The kids settled. Our routine clicked. We stopped feeling like we were on a long holiday and started feeling like we actually lived here.

"I realised the property was never really the decision. The area was the decision — and nobody had helped us make it properly."

That experience became the foundation of Phuket Living. Every client I work with starts with the same question: how do you actually want to live? The property comes second. The life comes first.

I'm not interested in being the biggest agent on the island. I'm interested in being the one families actually trust — the one their friends call when it's their turn to move.

What I believe

Five things that guide every conversation.

These aren't marketing principles. They're the ideas I quietly check every recommendation against — and the reason clients say working with me feels different.

01

Choosing the right area matters more than choosing the right property.

A great home in the wrong neighbourhood will never feel right. We start with lifestyle, not listings — every single time.

02

You don't need more options — you need the right ones.

I send clients three or four carefully matched properties, not forty. Decision fatigue is real and it leads to worse choices, not better ones.

03

Clarity removes fear.

Clear, honest information — even when it's not what you hoped to hear — is always more useful than reassurance. I'd rather lose a client to the truth than win one with a half-told story.

04

There is no "best" property — only what's right for your life.

I'm not here to sell the most expensive home. I'm here to find the most fitting one. Sometimes that's a ฿28,000 townhouse. Sometimes it's a beach villa. The price tag is rarely the point.

05

Relocating is a life decision, not a property transaction.

You're choosing a school run, a weekend pace, a community, a kitchen view at six in the morning. I treat the decision with the weight it deserves.

How I'm different

What Phuket Living is not.

It's easier to be honest about what you don't do than to claim to do everything. Here's what we deliberately aren't.

Not a listing aggregator. I don't publish everything available. I publish what I'd actually recommend — and only after understanding your situation. If you want every house on the island, there are bigger sites for that.

Not commission-driven in the traditional sense. My incentive is a client who settles well and refers others — not a fast close on the highest-value property. The fee structure is the same whether you spend ฿25,000 or ฿250,000.

Not a remote service. I accompany every viewing in person and answer every message myself. There are no virtual tours dressed up as local expertise, and no chatbot pretending to be me.

Not one-size-fits-all. A retired couple from Germany and a remote-working family from Sydney have almost nothing in common when it comes to the right area. The recommendation has to start from your life — not from what's currently on the market.
