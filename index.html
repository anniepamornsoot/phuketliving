How it works

Three steps. No overwhelm.

We move at your pace. You're never pressured into a viewing, a property, or a decision before it feels right.

01 Tell us how you want to live. Schools, lifestyle pace, budget, timeline. No form to fill — just a conversation with Annie.
02 We match you to the right areas and homes. A curated shortlist — not forty listings. Only properties that fit the life you described.
03 View in person, with Annie on the ground. She accompanies every viewing and answers questions in context — about the area, the build, the neighbours.
