Areas/Cherngtalay

Area guide · Northwest Phuket

Cherngtalay — where remote work meets island life.

Modern, well-connected, and built for people who actually need their internet to work. Cherngtalay sits next to Laguna, walkable to Boat Avenue, with the school cluster of Bangtao on its doorstep — and rents that are genuinely cheaper than its more famous neighbour.

Annie's honest take

"Cherngtalay is where I send couples and remote workers who want Bangtao's infrastructure without Bangtao's prices — or its school-run intensity. The internet is among the fastest on the island, Laguna's next door, and you're ten minutes from anywhere on the west coast that matters."

Daily life

What living here actually feels like.

Not the brochure version. The actual rhythm of a Tuesday in Cherngtalay — what you do, where you go, what's in walking distance and what isn't.

Morning routine

Coffee at Boat Avenue, breakfast meeting at one of the many cafés along the main road, or coworking at Garage Society. The pace is more Sydney-suburban than Phuket-touristy in the morning — laptops open, kids dropped, real work happening.

Schooling

UWC Thailand and BCIS are both within ten minutes — and unlike Bangtao, you're not on top of them. Cherngtalay sits at a comfortable distance: close enough for an easy school run, far enough that your weekend doesn't feel like an extension of school grounds.

Getting around

Mostly drivable. Boat Avenue and Porto de Phuket are walkable from many of the closer developments, but for everything else you'll want a car or scooter. Cherngtalay's main road can get busy in school hours and high season — most residents learn the back routes within a month.

Healthcare

Bangkok Hospital Phuket and BIMS are both within fifteen to twenty minutes. There are also smaller clinics inside Boat Avenue and along the main road for everyday care. Healthcare access is essentially identical to Bangtao without paying Bangtao prices for it.

Weekends

Layan Beach is eight minutes away — quieter and less developed than Bangtao Beach. Laguna's lakes for paddleboard and walks. Boat Avenue and Porto de Phuket cover dining and groceries. For a longer adventure, the airport is twenty minutes north and Phuket Town thirty south.

The honest picture

What Cherngtalay gets right — and what it doesn't.

Every area has trade-offs. These are Cherngtalay's, written plainly, so you can decide on the basis of what's actually true rather than what sounds good in a brochure.

What works well

  • Fastest internet on the island — fibre to most homes, ideal for remote work
  • Cheaper than Bangtao for equivalent space, often by 15–20%
  • Boat Avenue and Porto de Phuket walkable from many of the closer developments
  • Strong co-working scene — Garage Society, Hubba, plenty of café Wi-Fi
  • Schools are close without being on top of you
  • Layan Beach is quieter and less developed than Bangtao Beach

What to consider

  • Construction continues across many pockets — really worth checking the immediate neighbours of any home
  • Some developments are deep inland — not a "five-minute walk to the beach" area for many homes
  • Less walkable centre than Bangtao for non-driving routines
  • Main road traffic in school hours and high season
  • Newer area means less mature Thai community feel than Rawai or Kathu
  • Layan Beach access can be limited at certain points along the coast
