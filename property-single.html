About this home

A quiet villa, built for family weekdays.

This is one of the homes I'd recommend without hesitation to a family moving to Bangtao for the schools. The owner is a long-term resident who built the villa for his own family eight years ago — that shows in the small details: the kitchen island sized for breakfast for four, the shaded terrace that catches the afternoon breeze, the layout that lets the kids' bedrooms be closed off from the living areas in the evening.

The pool is genuinely usable — twelve metres long, with a shallow end, surrounded by mature gardens that screen it from neighbours. The garden itself is irrigated and maintained by the owner's gardener (included in the rent), so you won't be spending Saturdays on lawn care.

It's a five-minute drive to BCIS, eight to HeadStart Bangtao, and four minutes to Bangtao Beach itself. Bangkok Hospital Phuket is twenty minutes north. Boat Avenue and Porto de Phuket — the closest grocery and dining cluster — are a five-minute drive.

Available long-let from January, ideally to a family planning to stay at least 12 months. The owner has historically given a small discount for two-year leases.
